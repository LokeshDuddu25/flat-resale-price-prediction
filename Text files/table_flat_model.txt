Building type,Basic information
STANDARD,Basic or early HDB design with standard fittings and layouts. Usually the oldest model.
IMPROVED,Updated layout and separate bathrooms; more efficient use of space than Standard.
NEW GENERATION,Introduced in the 1980s with better ventilation and room layout.
MODEL A,Larger units with improved design and more spacious bedrooms and living areas.
MODEL A-MAISONETTE /IMPROVED-MAISONETTE, Two-storey flats with bedrooms upstairs and living space below. Highly valued for space.
APARTMENT / PREMIUM APARTMENT / PREMIUM MAISONETTE,Higher-end flats with better interior finishes and materials.
SIMPLIFIED,Compact and budget-friendly design with smaller space and combined bathrooms.
TERRACE,Rare groundfloor units with outdoor terrace areas.
ADJOINED FLAT,Two flats combined into a single or larger unit.
MULTI GENERATION / 3GEN,Designed for multi-generation families with additional rooms or separate living areas.
DBSS (Design Build and Sell Scheme),Built by private developers with modern facilities and premium finishes.
MODEL A2 / TYPE S1 / TYPE S2,Later variations of Model A and Executive flats with larger layouts and flexible design options.
PREMIUM APARTMENT LOFT,Luxury variant featuring high ceilings and open loft-style designs.
